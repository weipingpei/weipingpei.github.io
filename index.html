About Me

I am Weiping Pei, an Assistant Professor in the School of Cyber Studies at The University of Tulsa. I received my Ph.D degree in 2022 from Computer Science Department at the Colorado School of Mines, under the supervision of Professor Chuan Yue. Before that, I got my B.Eng. and M.Eng. degrees from Sun Yat-sen University. My research interests are broadly in the area of security and privacy, crowdsourcing, and machine learning.

I'm looking for self-motivated students (RA/TA support for both undergraduates and graduates) who are interested in doing research in security and privacy areas. Feel free to contact me.
